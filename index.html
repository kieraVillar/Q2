ABOUT US

   Cooldown Cafe was established during the late 1900s. As the times changed the menus have adapted to the changing tastes of the customers. The owner, Yuki Soma, was a Japanese who migrated here in the Philippines. The Cafe was named Cooldown as the country was in the tropical and is naturally hot especially during summers, the cafe was meant to be a place where people can relax and cooldown. It has always been the mission of the cafe to be able to fulfill the demands of their clients.
